About Chaniz Workers

Chaniz Workers is a digital platform created under Chaniz Company Ltd with the mission of connecting workers and employers across Tanzania. We aim to provide a simple, reliable, and modern way for people to find job opportunities and for companies to reach qualified workers quickly.

Founded in Dodoma, Ntyuka, our goal is to support local employment growth by offering digital tools that simplify recruitment, communication, and opportunity sharing. Whether you are an employer looking for skilled labor or a job seeker searching for your next opportunity, Chaniz Workers is here to help.

We also offer subscription options, giving users access to premium features such as unlimited job postings and priority listing placements. As the platform continues to grow, we aim to expand into training, worker verification, and more digital services to empower the Tanzanian workforce.

If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to contact us anytime using the details on the Contact page.
